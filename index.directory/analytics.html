MAE/MFE Analysis

Mean Adverse Excursion (MAE) and Mean Favorable Excursion (MFE) analysis requires intraday price data collection.

To enable this professional metric, add the following fields to your trade entries:

  • intraday_high - Highest price reached during the trade
  • intraday_low - Lowest price reached during the trade

This analysis helps identify if you're exiting trades too early or holding losers too long by comparing unrealized vs. realized P&L.
